Delivering Reliable Procurement, Contracting, and Energy Solutions Across Nigeria.

Established in 2012, Windek Procurement Services Limited is a fully registered Nigerian company providing end-to-end procurement, logistics, and general contracting services. With an operational base in Port Harcourt and a strong national footprint, we are strategically positioned to support Nigeria’s infrastructure and development priorities with excellence, transparency, and innovation.

For over a decade, Windek has delivered mission-critical supplies, energy infrastructure, and project management services for federal agencies, state governments, and corporate organizations. Our operations integrate engineering expertise, supply-chain precision, and sustainability practices to ensure every project is completed on time, within budget, and to international standards.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria’s leading procurement and contracting company, renowned for excellence, innovation, and sustainable service delivery.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, cost-effective, and innovative procurement and contracting solutions with the highest standards of professionalism and transparency.

Over the years, Windek has built a reputation for excellence through the execution of large-scale procurement and construction projects across Nigeria’s public and private sectors. Our project delivery model emphasizes professionalism, integrity, accountability, and stakeholder satisfaction.

We are driven by a vision to be Nigeria’s leading procurement and contracting company—renowned for innovation, transparency, and sustainable delivery. Every engagement reflects our core values of Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Partnership, and Accountability.
