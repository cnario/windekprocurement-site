Procurement Services

We specialize in sourcing and supplying high-quality goods, raw materials, equipment, and consumables for government and corporate clients. From ICT tools to safety gear and solar energy solutions, we ensure cost-effective, transparent, and timely delivery nationwide.

General Contracts

Our contracting division executes civil works, mechanical and electrical installations, building renovations, and infrastructure upgrades with strict adherence to safety, quality, and regulatory standards.

Project Management

Using PMI-aligned frameworks, we plan, coordinate, and execute complex projects from inception to completion. Our services cover logistics, vendor management, quality control, and performance tracking to ensure excellence and accountability.

Energy & Infrastructure

Through our integrated energy arm, we deliver upstream, midstream, and LPG infrastructure projects — including well services, terminal construction, and pipeline development — advancing Nigeria’s clean energy goals.

Engineering & Logistics

We provide end-to-end EPC solutions, fabrication, modular construction, and marine/land logistics support. Our approach ensures smooth material flow, safety compliance, and timely project delivery.

Consulting & Advisory

We offer procurement strategy, compliance advisory, and capacity building for organizations seeking to optimize their sourcing and contracting processes in line with best practices and federal regulations.

HSE & Compliance

Safety and environmental responsibility guide every project we undertake. We maintain ISO-aligned HSE systems, risk assessments, and zero-harm policies to protect people, assets, and the environment.

Facility Management

From maintenance and refurbishment to operational oversight, our facility management team ensures optimal performance and longevity for public and private infrastructure assets.

Get in Touch

We welcome partnerships, project inquiries, and collaboration opportunities. Whether it’s procurement, contracting, or infrastructure delivery — our team is ready to support your goals.
