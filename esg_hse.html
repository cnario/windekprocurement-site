Our ESG & HSE Commitment

At Windek Procurement Services Limited, sustainability and safety are not optional values — they are the foundation of how we operate. We integrate Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles with world-class Health, Safety, Security & Environment (HSE) standards to deliver projects that protect people, the planet, and prosperity.

Health, Safety, Security & Environment (HSSE)

We operate under a Zero Harm philosophy — ensuring every person returns home safely, every environment remains protected, and every operation meets regulatory standards. Our HSSE systems are ISO-aligned and rigorously audited for continuous improvement.

  • Comprehensive risk assessments and mitigation plans
  • Daily toolbox talks, safety drills, and compliance reviews
  • Emergency response and incident management systems
  • Secure project sites and logistics monitoring protocols
  • Zero major incident record since inception

Environmental Management

We take a proactive approach to environmental protection across all operations, ensuring efficiency, prevention, and restoration. Our goal is to reduce our ecological footprint while advancing Nigeria’s energy transition agenda.

  • Emission Control: Reducing carbon and GHG emissions across all assets
  • Waste Management: Recycling and safe disposal in line with national policy
  • Spill Prevention: Regular maintenance of tanks, pipelines, and storage facilities
  • Resource Efficiency: Optimized use of materials, fuel, and water
  • Site Remediation: Environmental audits and restoration post-project

Social Impact & Community Engagement

We are committed to empowering local communities and supporting inclusive development. Our social investment strategy focuses on education, skills, and infrastructure that create shared prosperity.

  • Local hiring and skill development initiatives
  • Scholarships and educational infrastructure support
  • Community infrastructure: roads, water, solar projects
  • Women & youth empowerment programs
  • Supplier and contractor training programs

By 2030, our goal is to impact over 50,000 Nigerians through direct community investments.

Governance & Ethical Leadership

Integrity and accountability are at the heart of Windek’s corporate governance framework. We comply with all BPP regulations and maintain full transparency across procurement and project execution.

  • Strict compliance with Nigerian public procurement laws
  • Zero-tolerance policy for corruption or unethical conduct
  • Regular internal audits and financial reviews
  • Open communication with regulators and stakeholders
  • Robust data security and privacy protocols

Sustainability Goals 2025 – 2030

Our sustainability roadmap integrates ESG principles into every project, driving long-term value creation for stakeholders and the environment.

  • Expand hybrid Solar + LPG energy infrastructure across three states
  • Reduce carbon intensity by 25% by 2030
  • Achieve ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 certifications
  • Digitize ESG data tracking and performance dashboards
  • Launch Windek Green Initiative for reforestation and clean energy access
